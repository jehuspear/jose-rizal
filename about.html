About Us

Welcome to our website dedicated to exploring the life, works, and writings of Jose Rizal, with a special focus on his essay Philippines a Century Hence. In compliance with Republic Act No. 1425 (the Rizal Law), our RIZLIFE subject, this school project aims to shed light on Rizal's visionary ideas and their impact on Philippine history.
